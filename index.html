Our Purpose

Pet adoption is incredibly important because it gives countless abandoned, neglected, or homeless animals a second chance at life in a loving home. Instead of supporting unethical breeding practices, adoption helps reduce overpopulation in shelters and rescues, ensuring that more animals find the care and companionship they deserve. At Coco Pets & Co., we are deeply committed to this cause. Our platform not only promotes pet adoption through awareness campaigns and partnerships with local shelters but also provides resources to guide new pet parents in creating a safe, happy environment for their adopted companions. By choosing adoption through Coco Pets & Co., you're not just gaining a pet‚Äîyou're saving a life and making space for another animal in need! Some common FAQs:

Why adopt?

Adopting a pet provides a second chance at life for an animal in need, helps reduce pet overpopulation, and offers numerous benefits for the adopter's well-being.

What are the adoption charges?

No external adoption fees are applicable, however, transporting fees might be charged by the owner.

Is there a minimum age to adopt?

Yes you must be atleast 18 years of age to adopt

Tips for first time pet owners

For first-time pet owners, preparation and patience are key. Start by ensuring your home is safe, establish a routine, and begin training and socialization early. Be prepared to invest time, effort, and potentially money into your new companion. Remember to prioritize your pet's health, and don't hesitate to seek help when needed.
